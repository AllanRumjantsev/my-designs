<p><b>Deadline management</b></p>
                                <p>For me, as a designer, everything usually depends on the deadline. The more time
                                    I have, the more I can play with different options, spend more time on research,
                                    and focus on smaller details. If there isn’t much time, then, as a rule, I use
                                    pre-made elements or templates and proven methods, which saves a lot of time.
                                </p>

                                <p>Usually, half of the success depends on the technical specifications. The more
                                    they are thought out, the better the chances are that everything will be
                                    completed on
                                    time. Even then, I tend to check along the way if we’re actually on the right
                                    track.</p>
