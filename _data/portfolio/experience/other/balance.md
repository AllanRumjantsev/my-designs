<p><b>Balance between preferences and tastes</b></p>

                                <p>It may sound great, that a designer has a good sense of taste, style and
                                    aesthetics.
                                    And it’s actually important. But I’ve found that more important for me, as a
                                    designer and a team player, is the ability to balance between my preferences, my
                                    team’s preferences, my client’s preferences and, at later stages, UX feedback.
                                </p>
